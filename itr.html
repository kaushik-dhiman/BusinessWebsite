INCOME TAX RETURN

Income Tax Return is the form in which assesse files information about your Income and tax thereon to Income Tax Department. Individuals who fulfil any one of the following conditions should by law file their Income Tax Returns during a financial year. The tax liability of a taxpayer is calculated based on his or her income. In case the tax is paid in excess during a year, then the individual/company will be eligible to receive an Income Tax Refund from the Income Tax Department.

Income Tax Return Forms

Various Income Tax Returns forms are ITR 1, ITR 2, ITR 3, ITR 4, ITR 5, ITR 6 and ITR 7. When you file a belated return, you are not allowed to carry forward certain losses.

Audits

Audit means an examination of the Income Tax Return filed by the company for the assessment year and inspecting the information mentioned in the ITR is true or not. A company is liable to perform a tax audit when it becomes liable under the corporate laws in India and cross a certain threshold.

Section 44AB of the Income Tax Act, 1961, lays down the provisions for an income tax audit of businesses in India. An external agency is to be appointed to assess the ITR Return filed by the company for the financial year and submitting the different tax audit forms with the tax authority.
